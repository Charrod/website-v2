Who is rLoop

rLoop is the world’s first non profit, open source, online think tank.

Formed in June of 2015 and incorporated as a non profit in September,

As a public benefit non profit corporation, rLoop truly is community focused. It’s about everyone being heard, and then rallying behind the best solution.

Coming together was our beginning, staying together has been our progress and working together with passion and trust is our success.
